My Algorithm

Without using a data set, the algorithm I developed looks at the probabilities of the user's past total right and left clicks and predicts the highest one.

Custom Navie Bayes

It is a partially customized version of the Navie Bayes algorithm. It first receives 49 clicks from the user on a 5xN matrix. Then the model is trained according to these clicks and Bayes theorem is applied. For 5 columns, probabilities are calculated and clicks are predicted according to the magnitude of the result.
